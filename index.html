Julia Culkin

Art Therapist

MPS, LCAT, ATR-P, CASAC-T trainee

I am a compassionate and contemporary practicing art therapist and portrait artist based in Brooklyn, New York. The process of making art is inherently healing, because it provides a creative structure for individuals to gain radical insight while redirecting negative energies and learning positive coping mechanisms. In my work I strive to give back by serving those who need it most, to help them process and surmount complex traumas, depression, anxiety, and substance abuse using mixed media art techniques individually or in group settings.
